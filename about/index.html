About

Paragraphle is a daily puzzle game based on Wikipedia™. Over 100,000 articles are available as part of the game, a subset that was chosen by picking the most linked-to articles within Wikipedia™ itself. Each article is broken into paragraphs and processed by an embedding model, which enables the game to compare the semantic similarity between any two chunks of text. This gives you a score that lets you know how close your guesses are to the article of the day.

The code behind this game, including the underlying data pipeline, is available on GitHub. If you encounter any issues or have feature suggestions you can submit them there!
