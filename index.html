Play Paragraphle, a daily game based on Wikipedia.

Each guess reveals new clues by matching paragraphs from your guess against the article of the day.

How to Play

Find the article of the day!

  • Each guess must be an existing Wikipedia article.
  • The game tells you how close you are to the article of the day.
  • Paragraphs from your guesses are ranked by how similar they are to the target.
  • The closer you get to zero, the better!
